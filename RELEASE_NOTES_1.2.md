# PiDay 1.2 — Release Notes

## What changed

### Featured-number switching (new)
- Added a true “active featured number” mode (π, π(4.16), τ, e, φ, Planck).
- Switching the featured number now updates **calendar**, **lookups**, **stats**, **sharing**, **widgets**, and **watch**.
- Quick switch: **long-press the top logo** to change featured number.
- Persistent switch: **Preferences → Featured Number**.

### Celebration popups + confetti (new)
- Featured-day confetti + short explanation popups:
  - π: March 14 (Pi Day)
  - π(4.16): April 16 (alternate Pi Day: 3.1416 ≈ π)
  - τ: June 28 (Tau Day)
  - e: February 7 (informal Euler’s number day)
  - φ: January 6 (informal golden ratio day)
  - Planck: April 14 (World Quantum Day tie-in)
- Bonus popup for π modes: **July 22 (22/7)** Pi approximation day.

### Bundled-only, review-friendly
- The shipped experience is bundled-first; copy no longer claims “live fallback” behavior as the primary flow.

## Versioning
- iOS: 1.2 (marketing version)
- Android: 1.2 (versionName)
