# PiDay 1.1 Release Notes

## App Store "What's New"

PiDay 1.1 is our nerdiest update yet.

- New Date Battles let you compare any two dates head-to-head and share the winner.
- Nerdy Stats 2.0 adds richer records, format analysis, Hall of Fame dates, and Pi Day specials.
- Saved Dates now works like a lightweight leaderboard, with better ranking and sorting.
- New share card styles let you post results in Classic, Nerd, or Battle form.
- Added more delightful easter eggs for Pi Day, leap day, repeated digits, and a few special free-search numbers.
- Polished widgets, watch details, and overall reliability across sharing and date lookups.

## Short Marketing Summary

PiDay 1.1 makes the app feel deeper, sharper, and more alive. You can now battle dates, explore a much richer stats experience, sort saved dates by how lucky they are in pi, and share better-looking result cards. We also added a layer of hidden delights for special dates and nerdy number searches.

## Release Highlights

- `Date Battles`
  Compare any two dates, see the winner, margin, winning format, and rarity, then share a battle card.

- `Nerdy Stats 2.0`
  Expanded stats now include Hall of Fame dates, calendar records, format upsets, sequence oddities, and yearly Pi Day comparisons.

- `Saved Dates Leaderboard`
  Saved dates can be sorted by best position, label, or calendar date, with inline rank, format, and rarity context.

- `New Share Styles`
  Share in Classic, Nerd, or Battle formats depending on how much flavor you want.

- `More Delight`
  Added special reactions for Pi Day, leap day, repeated-digit dates, very early and very deep hits, plus hidden free-search easter eggs.
